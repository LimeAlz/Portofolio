About Me

Today I learned

Today I Learned" (TIL) is a React.js-based platform where users share facts by category with credible sources. Users can upvote, downvote, or express amazement. Facts with more downvotes than upvotes and mind-blown reactions combined are flagged as potentially inaccurate. Built with React.js frontend and Supabase backend, TIL ensures a reliable, community-driven learning experience.
